Technology

Our Printing Technology

Our printing process is powered by modern eco-solvent technology, carefully chosen for its reliability, colour accuracy, and long-lasting performance. This enables us to deliver prints that retain their quality even in tough outdoor conditions — ideal for banners, vinyl, and sign boards.

At the core of our setup is the Allwin Konica 1024i Pro — a professional-grade eco-solvent printer known for consistent output and dependable performance. Combined with our experience since 2014, this technology helps us maintain uniform quality across every project, from single custom jobs to large bulk requirements.

We focus not just on printing, but on delivering results you can rely on.
