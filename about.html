Our Printing Technology

Our printing process is powered by modern eco-solvent technology, carefully chosen for its reliability, colour accuracy, and long-lasting performance. This enables us to deliver prints that retain their quality even in outdoor conditions, making them ideal for banners, vinyl, and sign boards.

At the core of our production setup is the Allwin Konica 1024i Pro, a professional-grade eco-solvent printer known for consistent output and dependable performance. Combined with our experience since 2014, this technology helps us maintain uniform quality across every project.

We focus not just on printing, but on delivering results that our customers can rely on — from single custom jobs to bulk requirements.
